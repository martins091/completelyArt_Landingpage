The Story Behind CompletelyArt

CompletelyArt was born from a simple but powerful idea: art should connect people, not confuse them. Eyitayo Olayeni-Dejo, an avid art lover and advocate for creativity, noticed a very recurring challenge, collectors often didn’t know which artists to trust, while talented creators struggled to find clients who truly valued their work. Frustrated by this divide, He set out to build a platform where both sides could meet with confidence and purpose.

Today, CompletelyArt is more than just a gallery, it’s a space where art lovers can discover pieces they can trust, and artists can showcase their creations to the right audience. Every interaction, every piece displayed, and every connection made is guided by a single mission: to make buying and selling art safe, seamless, and inspiring for everyone.

What started as a small idea has grown into a movement, bridging the gap between creators and collectors, and building a community where art truly belongs to everyone who loves it.
